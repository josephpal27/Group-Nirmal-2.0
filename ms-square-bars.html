MS Square bars

MS Square Bars are versatile steel bars with a uniform square cross-section. Rolled to perfection, they are available in a wide range of sizes, measured in millimetres. These steel bars offer excellent mechanical properties such as high tensile strength and good ductility, making them suitable for various applications. Their precise dimensions and smooth finish ensure ease of use and fabrication.

FEATURES

Material

MS Square Bars are made using MS Billets

Standards

MS Square Bars conform to IS Standards and are also available in customer specifications

Sizes

MS Square Bars are available in 27,32,33.5,40 mm

APPLICATIONS

Precast Concrete Reinforcement Components

MS Square Bars are used as reinforcement within precast concrete components like beams, columns, and slabs. Their square shape provides an excellent bond with the concrete, enhancing the structural integrity of the components.

Custom Machinery Frame Fabrication

MS Square Bars are welded and machined to create custom frames for machinery in industries such as agriculture, manufacturing, and mining. Their strength and rigidity ensure the stability of the machinery during operation.

Industrial Fencing and Gate Manufacturing

MS Square Bars are used to construct sturdy and durable industrial fencing and gates. Their square profile offers good resistance to impact and bending forces, providing security and protection for industrial sites.

Manufacturing of Furniture Components

MS Square Bars are utilised in the production of furniture components like table and chair legs, bed frames, and shelving units. They offer a modern and industrial aesthetic, while their strength and durability ensure long-lasting furniture.

Bridge Railings and Guardrail Construction

MS Square Bars are used to fabricate bridge railings and guardrails. Their strength and resistance to corrosion make them suitable for outdoor applications, providing safety and security for pedestrians and vehicles.

Staircase structures

MS Square Bars are used in the construction of sturdy and durable staircases. They are used to create the structural framework of the staircase, including the treads, risers, and handrails. Their strength and rigidity ensure the safety and stability of the staircase, making them ideal for both residential and commercial applications.
