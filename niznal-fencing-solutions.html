Niznal® Fencing Solutions

NIZNAL® fencing solutions offer a robust and durable option for various applications. Engineered with a unique zinc-aluminium alloy coating, NIZNAL® Chain link fences and Barbed wires exhibit exceptional corrosion resistance, surpassing traditional galvanised wire. This advanced coating provides superior protection against rust and weathering, ensuring the longevity of your fencing system. Beyond its durability, NIZNAL®’s versatile nature can be used in diverse settings, including residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Its strength and flexibility make it ideal for securing perimeters, creating enclosures, and reinforcing structures. NIZNAL® fencing solutions are a reliable and long-lasting option that enhances security and privacy.

FEATURES

Composition

NIZNAL® Chain Link fences and Barbed wires are made from premium steel wires coated with a 90%zinc and 10% aluminium alloy.

Diameter Range and Coating

NIZNAL® Chain Link fences and Barbed wires are available in wires ranging from 1.4-7mm with coatings ranging from 70-650 GSM

Tensile Strength

NIZNAL® Chain Link fences and Barbed wires display exceptional tensile strength ranging from 300-1900 MPa

Surface finish

NIZNAL® Chain Link fences and Barbed wires have a smooth and uniform surface finish that is free of defects or imperfections.

Standards

NIZNAL® Chain Link fences and Barbed wires are made according to a range of international standards which include ASTM, BS and DIN.

Packaging

NIZNAL® Chain Link fences and Barbed wires are packaged in led bundles.

APPLICATIONS

Residential Security

NIZNAL® fencing solutions can be used to create secure perimeters around residential properties. Its strength and durability deter intruders, while its aesthetic appeal enhances the property's appearance.

Commercial Property Security

NIZNAL® fencing solutions are ideal for securing commercial properties such as warehouses, factories, and office buildings. Its high tensile strength and corrosion resistance make it suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Industrial Site Security

NIZNAL® fencing solutions can be used to secure industrial sites, including power plants, refineries, and mining operations. Its robust construction and resistance to harsh weather conditions make it a reliable choice for industrial environments.

Perimeter Security for Government Facilities

NIZNAL® fencing solutions can be used to secure government facilities, such as military bases, government offices, and embassies. Its high security features, such as its strength and resistance to cutting and climbing, make it suitable for high-security applications.

Agricultural Fencing

NIZNAL® fencing solutions can be used to create enclosures for livestock, protect crops from wildlife, and secure agricultural property. Its durability and resistance to corrosion make it a long-lasting and reliable fencing solution for agricultural applications.
