Strong Barbed Wire
for Fencing

Barbed wire is a formidable security fence made of galvanized steel wire with sharp barbs. Its durability and flexibility are enhanced by the galvanization process, while the sharp barbs deter intruders. The wire's thickness, spacing, and barb shape can be customized to suit specific security needs, making it a versatile and effective security solution.

FEATURES

Material

Barbed wires are made from steel wire rods and are hot dip galvanised with zinc.

Standards

Barbed wires conform to IS 278

Barb Spacing

Spacing on the barbs vary between 3-6 inches

Tensile Strength

Tensile strength of out barbed wires vary between 300-550 MPa

Wire Diameter Range

Barbed wires are available in a variety of diameters ranging between 1.80-2.50 mm

Packaging

Barbed wires are packaged in coils weighing between 50-80 KGs

APPLICATIONS

Perimeter Security

Barbed wire is a common security measure used to protect properties. Its sharp points deter intruders, making it a reliable choice for securing homes, businesses, and critical infrastructure.

Animal Control

In agriculture, barbed wire is essential for controlling livestock. Its sharp edges prevent animals from escaping, ensuring they remain within designated areas.

Industrial Security

Barbed wire is used to secure industrial sites, warehouses, and storage facilities. It helps prevent unauthorized access and theft of valuable equipment and materials.

Military Applications

Barbed wire has a long history of military use. It creates obstacles and entanglements, hindering enemy advances and protecting strategic positions.

Construction Site Security

Barbed wire fencing safeguards construction sites. It deters theft and vandalism, protecting valuable equipment and materials.

Temporary Fencing

Barbed wire is a versatile solution for temporary fencing needs. It can be quickly installed and removed, making it ideal for various applications, such as crowd control and emergency situations.
