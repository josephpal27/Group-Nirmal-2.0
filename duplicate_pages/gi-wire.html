GI WIRES

Galvanized steel wires are manufactured from steel wire rods that are coated with zinc to enhance their corrosion resistance. The zinc, applied through hot-dip galvanization, acts as a barrier that protects the wire against rust. The thickness of the zinc coating is measured in grams per square meter (GSM), which determines the wire's corrosion resistance. We manufacture wires in various sizes that offer superior durability, tensile strength and malleability, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. GI Wires are widely used across different industries due to their cost-effectiveness, longevity and ease of handling.

APPLICATIONS

Fencing and perimeter protection

GI wires are used in constructing robust fences for residential, commercial, and industrial properties, ensuring security and privacy. They are ideal for perimeter fencing, security fencing, agricultural fencing, industrial fencing and residential fencing.

Power Industry

GI Wires are widely used in power transmission due to their high tensile strength and excellent corrosion resistance. They are commonly used as conductors for transmitting electricity and also as stay and earth wires to support transmission towers.

Agriculture and farming

GI wires are used in agriculture and poultry farming due to their durability and resistance to corrosion. They find use in supporting plants, tying branches, etc. In poultry farming, they are used to create sturdy cages and nest boxes, ensuring a safe and hygienic environment for the birds.

Solar fencing

GI wires are used in solar fencing due to their durability and resistance to corrosion. The galvanized coating on the wire protects it from the elements, ensuring a long lifespan for the fence. Additionally, GI wires are strong enough to withstand the tension required for effective solar fencing, making them a reliable choice for this application.

Reinforcing aluminium conductors

GI Wires are extremely suitable for reinforcing aluminium conductors. Their high tensile strength provides the conductors with the required strength to counteract mechanical stress incurred from stretching across long distances with minimal support.
