Read More About Niznal®

Niznal® wires by Group Nirmal are specialized for applications demanding high electrical conductivity and thermal resistance. Commonly used in transformer windings, power transmission, and coil applications, they offer precise dimensional control and uniform surface finish. These wires exhibit excellent strength-to-weight ratios and corrosion resistance, even in challenging environments.

Manufactured with an aluminium-nickel-zinc alloy blend, Niznal® combines the benefits of light weight and strong current carrying capacity. Whether used in utilities, heavy machinery, or control panels, Niznal® wires provide safe, energy-efficient performance.
