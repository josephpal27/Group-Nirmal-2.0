Niznal® Coated Wire

NIZNAL® Coated Wire is a durable, high-performance wire with a unique zinc-aluminium alloy coating. This innovative coating offers exceptional corrosion resistance, extending the wire's lifespan up to 6 times compared to traditional galvanised wires. Ideal for demanding outdoor applications, NIZNAL® is versatile and finds use in various sectors like fencing, agriculture, construction, and other industrial applications. Available in various sizes, it provides superior protection through a dual-layer mechanism, making it a reliable choice.

FEATURES

Composition

NIZNAL® Coated Wires are made from premium steel wires coated with a 90%zinc and 10%aluminium alloy.

Diameter Range and Coating

NIZNAL® Coated Wires are available in wires ranging from 1.4-7mm with coatings ranging from 70-650 GSM

Tensile Strength

NIZNAL® Coated Wires display exceptional tensile strength ranging from 300-1900 MPa

Surface Finish

NIZNAL® Coated Wires have a smooth and uniform surface finish that is free of defects or imperfections.

Standards

NIZNAL® Coated Wires are made according to a range of international standards which include ASTM, BS and DIN.

Packaging

NIZNAL® Coated Wires are packaged in 100-150 kg coils (upto 1000 KGs)

APPLICATIONS

Vineyard/Trellis Wire

NIZNAL® Coated Wires are durable and long lasting, and with their high tensile strength, the perfect choice for use in vineyards, orchards, etc. to support plant and fruit growth.

Chain link barbed wire and fences

Trusted for its durability. NIZNAL® Coated Wires offer robust solutions for barbed wire and chain link fences, providing long-lasting security and containment with minimal maintenance requirements.

Poultry

Ideal for poultry enclosures, NIZNAL® Coated Wires withstand the demanding conditions of poultry farming, offering corrosion resistance and structural integrity essential for ensuring the safety and well-being of poultry.

Smart fencing (weld mesh)

NIZNAL® Coated Wires form the backbone of smart fencing systems, delivering versatility and reliability for perimeter security and agricultural enclosures, supported by its self-healing properties and durability.

Gabion

NIZNAL® Coated Wires provide durable support for gabion structures resisting corrosion and maintaining stability in erosion control. retaining walls, and landscaping applications.

Rockfall mitigation

Critical for rockfall mitigation, Niznal® coated wires offer dependable containment and structural integrity. protecting infrastructure against falling rocks with its corrosion-resistant properties.
