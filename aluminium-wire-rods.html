Aluminium Wire Rods

Aluminium Wire Rods are highly-pure aluminium wires that are extruded into specific diameters and lengths. These rods serve as a critical raw material in various industries and roles, some of which include power transmission, automobile and engineering. Their excellent electrical conductivity, corrosion resistance, and lightweight properties make them ideal for a wide range of applications, such as electrical cables, automotive components, and conductors.

FEATURES

Material

Aluminium rods are made using pure aluminium (EC and T4 Alloy grade)

Standards

Aluminium rods conform to IS 9997

Wire Diameter Range

9.55MM (EC) 7.6 (T4 ALLOY)

Packaging

Aluminium rods are available in coil packing.

APPLICATIONS

Cable and conductor manufacturing

Aluminum rods are primarily used in manufacturing Aluminum conductors and power cables due to their excellent electrical conductivity, which is comparable to that of copper, but with significantly less weight and cost. The rods are produced through continuous casting and rolling and are then fed into drawing machines that gradually reduce their diameter to form wires. These wires are often stranded together to improve flexibility and increase the current-carrying capacity of the final cables and conductors.
