LT PVC Insulated Power Cables

LT PVC Power cables are electrical cables designed for low-voltage power distribution. They typically consist of one or more copper conductors insulated with PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) and encased in a PVC outer sheath. The conductors may be solid or stranded, and the number of conductors can vary depending on the cable's specific application. These cables are commonly used for indoor and outdoor installations, providing reliable power transmission in various settings.

FEATURES

Material and insulation

PVC Power Cables are made using premium quality aluminium with class 2 stranding. Available in 2 insulations- General Purpose (Type A) and Heat resistant (Type C). The outer sheath of the cable is made using PVC Type ST 2/ST 1/FRLS/FR/LSZH.

Configuration

PVC Power Cables are available in multiple strand configurations.

Sizes

PVC Power Cables are available in various sizes ranging between 1.5 square mm- 1000 square mm

Standards

PVC Power Cables conform to IS:1554 Part 1: 1988

Voltage Rating

PVC Power Cables are rated for 1.1KV

Temperature Rating

Temperature rating of PVC Power Cables range between 70 degrees celsius(general purpose insulation) and 85 degrees celsius (heat resistant insulation) under normal conditions. During overload, these cables can operate up to 160 degrees celsius(general and heat resistant insulation).

APPLICATIONS

Large Scale Power Distribution

LT PVC cables are used in substations and distribution networks to connect transformers, switchgear, and other electrical equipment, ensuring reliable and efficient power delivery to homes, businesses, and industries.

Large Scale Industrial Facilities

These cables are extensively used within factories and plants to power heavy machinery, motors, conveyors, and other critical equipment, providing the necessary electrical infrastructure for industrial operations.

Commercial Buildings

LT PVC cables are essential for the internal wiring of commercial structures, powering lighting systems, air conditioning, elevators, and other electrical appliances and systems within offices, malls, hotels, and other commercial establishments.
