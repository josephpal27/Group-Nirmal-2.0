Secure
Chain Link Fencing

Chain link fences are durable, galvanised steel fences with a diamond-shaped mesh. They offer a balance of security and visibility, making them suitable for various applications. These fences are easy to install and maintain, ensuring long-lasting performance and affordability. They are commonly used in commercial, industrial, and residential settings.

FEATURES

Material

Chain link fences are made from premium steel wire rods and are hot dip galvanised with zinc.

Standards

Chain link fences conform to IS 278

Wire Diameter Range

Chain link fences are available in a variety of diameters ranging between 1.80-2.50

Spacing

Sizing of the diamonds vary between 3-6 inches

Tensile Strength

Tensile strength of out barbed wires vary between 300-550 MPa

Packaging

Barbed wires are available in coils weighing between 50-80 KGs

APPLICATIONS

Perimeter Security

Chain link fences are widely used to secure perimeters of residential, commercial, and industrial properties, providing a physical barrier against unauthorised access and potential threats.

Sports Field Enclosures

Chain link fences are commonly used to enclose sports fields, such as baseball diamonds, soccer fields, and tennis courts, ensuring the ball stays within the playing area and preventing spectators from entering the field.

Industrial fencing

Chain link fences are versatile and durable, used for various applications beyond security. They're ideal for shipyard fencing due to their strength, visibility, and ease of installation. They are an effective deterrent against all kinds of intrusions.

Animal Enclosures

Chain link fences are ideal for containing animals in zoos, farms, and animal shelters. They offer good visibility, allowing for easy monitoring of the animals, and are durable enough to withstand the rigours of animal activity.

Construction Site Security

Chain link fences are used to secure construction sites, preventing unauthorised entry and protecting valuable equipment and materials from theft or vandalism.

Temporary Fencing

Chain link fences are often used for temporary fencing solutions, such as crowd control at events, construction site barriers, and security perimeters during special events.
